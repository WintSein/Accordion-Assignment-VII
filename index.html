Elements of the Periodic Table

Diamond is a form of carbon, and it is the hardest natural material known. Diamonds are highly valued for their brilliance and beauty.

Gold is a precious metal with high economic and cultural value. It is soft, shiny, and malleable, often used in jewelry and electronics.

Silver is a soft, white, lustrous metal with the highest electrical and thermal conductivity of any metal. It is often used in coins, jewelry, and electronics.

Platinum is a dense, malleable, and highly unreactive metal. It is used in jewelry, medical equipment, and as a catalyst in chemical reactions.
